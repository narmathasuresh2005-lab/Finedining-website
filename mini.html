Mini Crab Cakes are delicately crisp on the outside with a tender, flavorful center. Made with fresh crab meat and subtle seasonings, each bite highlights the natural sweetness of the seafood.

Key Ingredients:

Fresh crab meat, Bread crumbs, Egg, Mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, Lemon juice, Fresh parsley, Black pepper powder, Garlic, Onion, Salt, Paprika, Oil.

Quantity: 21 cakes

No. of Orders

Description

1. Fresh crab meat is carefully checked to remove any shells.
2. Crab meat is placed in a bowl and gently flaked to maintain texture.
3. Bread crumbs are added to help bind the mixture without overpowering the crab.
4. Egg and mayonnaise are mixed in to provide moisture and richness.
5. Dijon mustard and lemon juice are added for mild tang and freshness.
6. Minced garlic and finely chopped onion are incorporated for depth of flavor.
7. Parsley, paprika, black pepper, and salt are added to season the mixture.
8. The mixture is gently folded to keep the crab meat intact.
9. Small portions are shaped into evenly sized mini cakes.
10. The crab cakes are chilled briefly to help them hold their shape.
11. Butter or oil is heated in a pan over medium heat.
12. Crab cakes are pan-fried until golden brown and crisp on both sides.
13. Once cooked, they are drained lightly and rested before serving.
14. Served warm with lemon wedges or a light dipping sauce.
