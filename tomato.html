Cherry Tomato and Red Pepper Soup is a smooth, vibrant blend of naturally sweet tomatoes and gently roasted red peppers.Silky in texture and rich in aroma, it’s a bowl of pure comfort. A wholesome starter crafted to soothe and satisfy.

Key Ingredients:

Cherry tomatoes, Red bell peppers, Onion, Garlic cloves, Olive oil, Vegetable stock, Fresh basil leaves, Black pepper powder, Paprika, Salt, Cream (for garnish).

Quantity: 500ml (1 Bowl)

No. of Orders

Description

1. Cherry tomatoes are washed thoroughly and kept whole for roasting.
2. Red bell peppers are deseeded and cut into large pieces.
3. Tomatoes and peppers are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
4. They are roasted until soft and lightly charred to enhance sweetness.
5. Olive oil is heated in a pot and chopped onions are sautéed until translucent.
6. Garlic is added and cooked gently until aromatic.
7. Roasted tomatoes and peppers are added to the pot.
8. Vegetable stock is poured in to create the soup base.
9. Paprika and black pepper are added for warmth and depth.
10. The mixture is simmered gently to allow flavors to blend.
11. Fresh basil leaves are stirred in for aroma and freshness.
12. The soup is blended until smooth and velvety.
13. The consistency is adjusted with stock if required.
14. Finished with a drizzle of cream or olive oil before serving.
