Description

1. Large button mushrooms are cleaned gently and stems are removed.
2. Mushroom stems are finely chopped and kept aside for the filling.
3. Olive oil or butter is heated in a pan over medium heat.
4. Chopped onions and garlic are sautéed until soft and aromatic.
5. Finely chopped mushroom stems are added and cooked until moisture reduces.
6. Cream cheese is mixed in to create a rich, smooth filling.
7. Grated mozzarella or Parmesan is added for flavor and melt.
8. Bread crumbs are folded in to add texture and structure.
9. Parsley, herbs, black pepper, and salt are added for seasoning.
10. The prepared stuffing is spooned generously into each mushroom cap.
11. Stuffed mushrooms are arranged on a greased baking tray.
12. They are baked until the tops turn lightly golden and bubbly.
13. TOnce cooked, the mushrooms are rested briefly before serving.
14. Served warm as a flavorful and elegant starter.
