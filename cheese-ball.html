Creamy and Crunchy Cheese Balls are a classic appetizer crafted to deliver comfort and indulgence in every bite. With a crisp golden exterior and a rich, molten cheese center, this dish offers a perfect balance of texture and flavor.

Key Ingredients:

Mozzarella cheese, Cheddar cheese, Boiled potatoes, Bread crumbs, Cornflour, All-purpose flour (maida), Garlic, Black pepper powder, Mixed herbs, Salt, Oil (to fry)

Quantity: 15 pieces

No. of Orders

Description

1. Fresh mozzarella and cheddar cheese are finely grated to ensure a smooth and creamy texture.
2. Boiled potatoes are mashed until soft, helping bind the mixture perfectly.
3. Grated cheese is combined with mashed potatoes in a mixing bowl.
4. Minced garlic, black pepper, mixed herbs, and salt are added to enhance flavor.
5. Cornflour and all-purpose flour are mixed in to give structure and firmness.
6. The mixture is kneaded gently until it becomes smooth and easy to shape.
7. Small portions are rolled into evenly sized balls for consistent cooking.
8. Each ball is coated with a thin slurry and then rolled in bread crumbs for extra crunch.
9. The prepared cheese balls are rested briefly to help the coating set properly.
10. Oil is heated to the ideal temperature for deep frying.
11. Cheese balls are carefully lowered into hot oil and fried until golden brown.
12. Once crisp and perfectly cooked, they are removed and drained on absorbent paper.
13. Served hot to enjoy the crunchy exterior and creamy, melted cheese center.
