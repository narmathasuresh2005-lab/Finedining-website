Kerala Style Pepper Chicken is a fiery and aromatic delicacy inspired by the spice-rich kitchens of South India. A rustic classic crafted for true spice lovers.

Key Ingredients:

Chicken, Black peppercorns, Onion, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Curry leaves, Coriander powder, Turmeric powder, Garam masala, Fennel seeds, Coconut oil, Vinegar, Salt.

Quantity: 1 bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Chicken is cleaned thoroughly and cut into medium pieces.
2. Black peppercorns are freshly crushed for maximum aroma.
3. Coconut oil is heated in a pan or kadai.
4. Fennel seeds are added and lightly sautéed.
5. Sliced onions are added and cooked until golden brown.
6. They are dipped into beaten eggs evenly.
7. Curry leaves are added for authentic aroma.
8. Turmeric and coriander powder are mixed in gently.
9. Chicken pieces are added and tossed to coat with spices.
10. Salt is added and mixed thoroughly.
11. The pan is covered and chicken is cooked on low heat.
12. Freshly crushed black pepper is added generously.
13. Garam masala is sprinkled for depth of flavor.
14. The dish is cooked until chicken is tender and peppery.
15. Finished with a dash of vinegar or lemon juice.
16. Served hot, garnished with curry leaves.
