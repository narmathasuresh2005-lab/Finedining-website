Tandoori Chicken is a timeless classic marinated in spiced yogurt and cooked to perfection in a traditional tandoor.

Key Ingredients:

Chicken, Thick yogurt, Ginger-garlic paste, Kashmiri red chili powder, Turmeric powder, Coriander powder, Garam masala, Lemon juice, Mustard oil, Tandoori masala, Salt, Oil.

Quantity: 5 pieces

No. of Orders

Description

1. Chicken is cleaned thoroughly and deep slits are made for marination.
2. Lemon juice and salt are rubbed onto the chicken for pre-marination.
3. Yogurt is whisked until smooth in a mixing bowl.
4. Ginger-garlic paste and spices are added to the yogurt.
5. Mustard oil is mixed in to enhance flavor and aroma.
6. The marinade is applied generously to the chicken.
7. Chicken is rested for several hours for deep flavor infusion.
8. Marinated chicken is placed on skewers or a grilling tray.
9. It is cooked in a tandoor or oven at high heat.
10. Chicken is turned occasionally for even charring.
11. Butter or oil is brushed during cooking for juiciness.
12. Chicken is cooked until tender inside and smoky outside.
13. Rested briefly to retain moisture.
14. Served hot with onion salad, lemon wedges, and mint chutney
