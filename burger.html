Crispy Fried Chicken Burger is stacked with a golden-fried, juicy chicken fillet layered inside a soft toasted bun. A burger made for true comfort-food lovers.

Key Ingredients:

Boneless chicken fillet, Yogurt, Garlic powder, Paprika, Black pepper, Salt, All-purpose flour, Cornflour, Oil, Burger buns, Lettuce leaves, Tomato slices, Onion slices, Cheese slice, Mayonnaise, Potatoes, Butter

Quantity: 1 large burger

No. of Orders

Description

1. Chicken fillets are cleaned and tenderized.
2. Fillets are marinated in buttermilk with spices.
3. Flour and cornflour are mixed with seasoning.
4. Chicken is coated thoroughly in the flour mixture.
5. Oil is heated to the correct frying temperature.
6. Chicken is deep-fried until golden and crispy.
7. Fried chicken is rested to keep it juicy inside.
8. Potatoes are sliced thin for chips.
9. Chips are fried until crisp and lightly golden.
10. Burger buns are sliced and lightly buttered.
11. Buns are toasted until soft and golden.
12. Sauce is spread evenly on the bun base.
13. Lettuce, tomato, and onion are layered.
14. Crispy chicken is placed on top and cheese added if desired.
15. Burger is closed, plated with potato chips, and served hot.
