Indian Mixed Vegetable Curry with Chapati

Indian Mixed Vegetable Curry is a wholesome medley of fresh vegetables simmered in a rich, aromatic gravy.

Key Ingredients:

Mixed vegetables (carrot, potato, beans, peas, cauliflower), Tomato, Turmeric powder, Red chili powder, Fresh coconut, Coriander powder, Salt, Fresh coriander leaves, Oil, Whole wheat flour.

Quantity: 3 chapati, 1 bowl curry

No. of Orders

Description

1. All vegetables are washed, peeled, and cut into even pieces.
2. Oil is heated in a pan.
3. Cumin seeds are added and allowed to crackle.
4. Chopped onions are sautéed until golden.
5. Ginger-garlic paste and green chilies are added and cooked well.
6. Tomatoes are added and cooked into a thick masala base.
7. Turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder are mixed in.
8. Mixed vegetables are added and stirred gently.
9. Salt is added and vegetables are sautéed briefly.
10. Water is added and the curry is simmered until vegetables are tender.
11. Garam masala is added for final flavor.
12. Curry is garnished with fresh coriander leaves.
13. Whole wheat flour is kneaded into a soft dough.
14. Chapatis are rolled out and cooked on a hot tawa.
15. Chapatis are lightly roasted with ghee and served hot with curry.
