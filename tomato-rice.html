Tomato Rice

Tomato Rice is a vibrant and comforting dish made with fragrant rice and tangy, spiced tomato masala. A classic South Indian favorite loved for its simplicity and taste.

Key Ingredients:

Basmati, Ripe tomatoes, Onion, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Mustard seeds, Cumin seeds, Curry leaves, Turmeric powder, Red chili powder, Fresh coconut, Coriander powder, Salt, Fresh coriander leaves, Oil.

Quantity: 1 Bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Rice is washed thoroughly and cooked until fluffy.
2. Tomatoes are chopped or blended into a smooth purée.
3. Oil is heated in a pan or cooker.
4. Mustard and cumin seeds are tempered until aromatic.
5. Curry leaves are added for fragrance.
6. Sliced onions are sautéed until soft and translucent.
7. Ginger-garlic paste and green chilies are cooked well.
8. Tomato purée is added and simmered until thick.
9. Turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder are mixed in.
10. Salt is added and adjusted to taste.
11. Cooked rice is added to the tomato masala.
12. Rice is mixed gently to coat evenly.
13. The dish is cooked briefly to absorb flavors.
14. Garnished with fresh coriander leaves.
15. Served hot with raita or papad.
