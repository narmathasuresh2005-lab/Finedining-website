Channa Masala with Chapati is a wholesome combination of bold spices and comforting flavors. Slow-cooked chickpeas simmered in a rich onion-tomato gravy create a deeply satisfying curry.

Key Ingredients:

Chickpeas, Onion, Tomato, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Channa masala powder, Turmeric powder, Cumin seeds, Coriander powder, Red chili powder, Garam masala, Salt, Ghee, Whole wheat flour.

Quantity: 3 chapati & 1 bowl of Channa

No. of Orders

Description

1. Chickpeas are soaked overnight and pressure cooked until soft.
2. Oil is heated in a pan and cumin seeds are added until aromatic.
3. Finely chopped onions are sautéed until golden brown.
4. Ginger-garlic paste and green chilies are added and cooked well.
5. Tomato puree is added and simmered until oil separates.
6. Turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and channa masala powder are mixed in.
7. Cooked chickpeas are added and stirred to coat with the masala.
8. Water is added and the curry is simmered until thick and flavorful.
9. Garam masala is sprinkled and mixed gently.
10. Fresh coriander leaves are added for aroma and garnish.
11. For chapathi, whole wheat flour is kneaded with water and salt into a soft dough.
12. The dough is rested and divided into equal portions.
13. Each portion is rolled into thin circles.
14. Chapathis are cooked on a hot tawa until soft with light brown spots.
15. Served hot together for a complete, comforting meal.
