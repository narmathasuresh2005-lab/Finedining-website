Chicken Dumplings are filled with juicy minced chicken wrapped in delicate, soft dough. Steamed to perfection, they deliver rich flavor with every bite. Steamed to perfection, they deliver rich flavor with every bite. A comforting classic loved by dumpling enthusiasts.

Key Ingredients:

Minced chicken, Garlic, Ginger, Spring onions, Soy sauce, Black pepper, Sesame oil, Salt, All-purpose flour, soy dipping sauce.

Quantity: 10 Dumplings

No. of Orders

Description

1. Minced chicken is placed in a mixing bowl.
2. Garlic, ginger, and spring onions are added.
3. Soy sauce, sesame oil, pepper, and salt are mixed in.
4. Filling is mixed well until smooth and juicy.
5. Dumpling wrappers are prepared or rolled thin.
6. A spoonful of filling is placed in the center.
7. Water is boiled in a steamer.
8. Potatoes are sliced thin for chips.
9. Dumplings are arranged without touching each other.
10. Dumplings are steamed until fully cooked.
11. Wrappers turn glossy and slightly translucent.
12. Dumplings are removed gently from the steamer.
13. Dipping sauce is prepared separately.
14. Dumplings are plated neatly.
15. Served hot with sauce on the side.
