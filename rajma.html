Rajma Masala with Rice is a soulful North Indian classic that brings comfort to every bite. Slow-cooked kidney beans simmered in a rich, spiced tomato-onion gravy create deep, homely flavors.

Key Ingredients:

Kidney beans, Onion, Tomato, Ginger-garlic paste, Red chili powder, Green chilies, Cumin seeds, Coriander powder, Turmeric powder, Garam masala, Fresh coriander leaves, Kasuri methi, Salt, Basmati rice, Oil.

Quantity: 1 bowl rajma with 1 bowl rice

No. of Orders

Description

1. Rajma beans are soaked overnight and pressure cooked until soft and tender.
2. Oil or ghee is heated in a pan and cumin seeds are added until aromatic.
3. Finely chopped onions are sautéed until golden brown.
4. Ginger-garlic paste and green chilies are added and cooked well.
5. Tomato puree is added and simmered until oil begins to separate.
6. Turmeric, coriander powder, and red chili powder are mixed in.
7. Cooked rajma is added and stirred gently into the masala.
8. Water is added to achieve the desired gravy consistency.
9. The curry is simmered slowly to allow flavors to deepen.
10. Garam masala and kasuri methi are added for aroma.
11. Fresh coriander leaves are added and mixed gently.
12. For rice, basmati rice is washed and soaked briefly.
13. Rice is cooked in boiling salted water until fluffy and separate.
14. The rajma masala is rested briefly before serving.
15. Served hot with steamed rice for a complete, comforting meal.
