Brown Bread Ice Cream

Brown Bread Ice Cream is a delightful fusion dessert that combines creamy ice cream with toasted brown bread crumbs. Rich, nutty, and smooth, it offers a unique balance of crunch and creaminess. Lightly sweet with a comforting aroma, this dessert is both nostalgic and indulgent. A must-try treat with a modern twist.

Key Ingredients:

Brown bread slices, Fresh cream, Sugar, Butter, Marshmallows, Condensed milk, Vanilla essence, Nuts.

Quantity: 150g pieces

No. of Orders

Description

1. Brown bread slices are crumbled finely.
2. Butter is heated in a pan on low flame.
3. Bread crumbs are toasted until golden and aromatic.
4. Toasted crumbs are cooled completely.Chocolate is added and stirred until fully melted.
5. Fresh cream is chilled before use.
6. Cream is whipped until soft peaks form.
7. Milk and sugar are mixed into the cream.
8. Vanilla essence is added for flavor.
9. Toasted bread crumbs are folded gently into the mixture.
10. Condensed milk is added if extra richness is desired.
11. Mixture is poured into a freezer-safe container.
12. Container is covered tightly.
13. Ice cream is frozen until set.
14. Scoops are formed carefully.
