Chicken Keema with Naan is a hearty North Indian favorite made with finely minced chicken cooked in aromatic spices.

Key Ingredients:

Minced chicken,Onion, Tomato, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Turmeric powder, Red chili powder, Coriander powder, Garam masala, Cumin seeds, Oil, Salt, Fresh coriander leaves, All-purpose flour, Yeast.

Quantity: 3 Naan with 1 serve

No. of Orders

Description

1. Minced chicken is cleaned and kept aside.
2. Dough for naan is prepared and rested.
3. Oil is heated in a pan.
4. Cumin seeds are added and allowed to crackle.
5. Onions are sautéed until golden brown.
6. Ginger-garlic paste and green chilies are added and cooked well.
7. Tomatoes are added and cooked into a thick gravy base.
8. Turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder are mixed in.
9. Minced chicken is added and stirred thoroughly.
10. Keema is cooked on medium heat until juicy and tender.
11. Water is added if needed to balance moisture.
12. Garam masala is added for final aroma.
13. Fresh coriander leaves are sprinkled on top.
14. Naan is rolled and cooked in a tandoor or hot pan.
15. Naan is brushed with butter and served hot with keema.
