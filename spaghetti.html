Vegetable Spaghetti is a wholesome blend of perfectly cooked pasta and garden-fresh vegetables. A classic choice crafted for those who enjoy light yet hearty meals.

Key Ingredients:

Spaghetti pasta, Olive oil, Garlic, Onion, Bell peppers (red, yellow, green), Zucchini, Broccoli, Sweet corn, Cherry tomatoes, Black pepper powder, Chili flakes, Dried oregano, Fresh basil leaves, Salt.

Quantity: 1 bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Spaghetti is boiled in salted water until perfectly al dente.
2. Cooked pasta is drained and kept aside with a little reserved water.
3. Olive oil is heated in a pan over medium heat.
4. Garlic is sautéed gently until aromatic.
5. Sliced onions are added and cooked until soft.
6. Bell peppers, zucchini, and broccoli are added and sautéed lightly.
7. Sweet corn and cherry tomatoes are mixed in for sweetness and color.
8. Black pepper, chili flakes, and oregano are added for seasoning.
9. Cooked spaghetti is added to the pan and tossed gently.
10. Reserved pasta water is added to bring everything together.
11. Fresh basil leaves are folded in for aroma.
12. The spaghetti is cooked briefly to absorb flavors.
13. Adjust seasoning and drizzle with olive oil.
14. Served hot, finished with grated cheese if desired.
