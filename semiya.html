Mixed Vegetable Semiya Kichadi is a light and nourishing South Indian comfort dish made with roasted vermicelli and fresh vegetables.

Key Ingredients:

Vermicelli, Curry leaves, Mixed vegetables (carrot, potato, beans, peas), Onion, Fresh coriander leaves, Turmeric powder, Green chilies, Salt, Ginger, Chana dal, Mustard seeds, Oil, Urad dal, Cumin seeds.

Quantity: 1 bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Semiya is dry-roasted until lightly golden and aromatic.
2. Oil or ghee is heated in a pan or kadai.
3. Mustard seeds are added and allowed to splutter.
4. Cumin seeds, urad dal, and chana dal are added and roasted.
5. Curry leaves are added for aroma.
6. Chopped onions are sautéed until soft.
7. Ginger and green chilies are added and cooked gently.
8. Mixed vegetables are added and sautéed briefly.
9. Turmeric and salt are added and mixed well.
10. Water is added and brought to a gentle boil.
11. Roasted semiya is added slowly while stirring.
12. The mixture is cooked on low heat until soft.
13. The pan is covered to allow steaming.
14. Garnished with fresh coriander leaves.
15. Served hot with coconut chutney or pickle.
