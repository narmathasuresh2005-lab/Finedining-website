Sticky Garlic Chicken Noodles bring together tender chicken, glossy noodles, and an irresistible garlic-rich sauce. A street-style favorite crafted for serious noodle lovers.

Key Ingredients:

Egg noodles, Boneless chicken, Garlic, Ginger, Spring onions, Capsicum, Soy sauce, Chili sauce, Tomato ketchup, Oyster sauce, Vinegar, Black pepper, Cornflour, Oil, Salt.

Quantity: 1 bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Noodles are boiled in salted water until just cooked..
2. Cooked noodles are drained and tossed with a little oil.
3. Chicken is sliced thin and lightly coated with cornflour.
4. Oil is heated in a wok on high flame.
5. Chicken is stir-fried until golden and cooked through.
6. Chicken is removed and kept aside.
7. More oil is added and garlic is sautéed until aromatic.
8. Ginger and spring onions are added and tossed quickly.
9. Capsicum or vegetables are added for crunch.
10. Soy sauce, chili sauce, ketchup, and vinegar are mixed in.
11. The sauce is simmered until thick and sticky.
12. Chicken is added back and coated evenly.
13. Cooked noodles are added and tossed on high heat.
14. Black pepper and salt are adjusted carefully.
15. Finished with spring onion greens and served hot.
