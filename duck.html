Crispy and Juicy Duck Roast is a luxurious dish where perfectly roasted duck meets bold, aromatic spices. A refined classic crafted for true gourmet enthusiasts.

Key Ingredients:

Duck, Ginger-garlic paste, Onion, Black pepper, Red chili powder, Coriander powder, Garam masala, Soy sauce, Honey, Fresh herbs, Salt, Oil

Quantity: 1 whole duck

No. of Orders

Description

1. Duck is cleaned thoroughly and excess fat is trimmed carefully.
2. The skin is lightly scored to help render fat evenly.
3. Ginger-garlic paste and spices are mixed into a marinade.
4. Duck is rubbed generously with the spice mixture.
5. The duck is rested to allow flavors to penetrate deeply.
6. A roasting pan is preheated with butter or duck fat.
7. Duck is placed skin-side up in the pan.
8. It is roasted slowly at moderate temperature.
9. Fat released during roasting is basted over the duck.
10. The skin turns golden and crispy with continuous basting.
11. Honey or glaze is brushed for shine and caramelization.
12. Duck is cooked until the meat becomes juicy and tender.
13. Rested briefly after roasting to retain moisture.
14. Sliced carefully and plated elegantly.
15. Served hot with a rich sauce or seasonal sides.
