Wheat Kichadi is a wholesome South Indian comfort dish made from cracked wheat, lentils, and fresh vegetables.

Key Ingredients:

Broken wheat, Moong dal, Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas), Onion, Ginger, Turmeric powder, Green chilies, Green chilies, Mustard seeds, Oil, Urad dal, Cumin seeds,Curry leaves, Salt.

Quantity: 1 bowl Kichadi

No. of Orders

Description

1. Broken wheat is washed thoroughly and drained.
2. Moong dal is rinsed and kept aside.
3. Ghee or oil is heated in a pressure cooker or heavy pot.
4. Mustard seeds are added and allowed to splutter.
5. Cumin seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves are added.
6. Chopped onions are sautéed until soft and translucent.
7. Ginger and green chilies are added for aroma.
8. Mixed vegetables are added and sautéed lightly.
9. Broken wheat and moong dal are added and mixed well.
10. Turmeric and salt are added and stirred evenly.
11. Water is added in the correct proportion.
12. The mixture is pressure-cooked until soft and mushy.
13. The kichadi is rested briefly to allow flavors to settle.
14. Coconut chutney is freshly ground and lightly tempered.
15. Sambar is slow-cooked with dal, vegetables, and spices.
16. Wheat kichadi is served hot with chutney and sambar.
