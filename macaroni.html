Masala Macaroni

Masala Macaroni is a delicious fusion of classic pasta and bold Indian spices. Tossed with fresh vegetables and a rich masala base, every bite delivers warmth and comfort.

Key Ingredients:

Macaroni pasta, Onion, Tomato, Oil, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Capsicum (bell peppers), Carrot, Turmeric powder, Red chili powder, Garam masala, Coriander powder, Tomato ketchup, Black pepper, Fresh coriander leaves, Salt.

Quantity: 1 Bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Macaroni is boiled in salted water until perfectly cooked.
2. Cooked pasta is drained and kept aside.
3. Oil is heated in a pan.
4. Chopped onions are sautéed until soft.
5. Ginger-garlic paste and green chilies are added and cooked well.
6. Tomatoes are added and cooked into a thick masala base.
7. Turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala are added.
8. Chopped vegetables are added and sautéed gently.
9. Salt and black pepper are added for seasoning.
10. Cooked macaroni is added to the masala.
11. Everything is tossed gently to coat the pasta evenly.
12. Tomato ketchup is added for slight tang and sweetness.
13. The dish is cooked briefly to absorb flavors.
14. Garnished with fresh coriander leaves.
15. Served hot as a hearty snack or main dish.
