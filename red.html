Red Sauce Pasta is a vibrant blend of rich tomato flavors and aromatic herbs. Slow-simmered sauce coats every strand of pasta, delivering warmth and comfort in each bite.

Key Ingredients:

Pasta, Ripe tomatoes or tomato puree, Ginger, Onion, Garlic, Olive oil, Red chili flakes, Black pepper powder, Dried oregano, Dried basil, Fresh basil leaves, Sugar, Salt, Parmesan cheese.

Quantity: 250g

No. of Orders

Description

1. Pasta is boiled in salted water until perfectly al dente.
2. The cooked pasta is drained and kept aside with a little reserved water.
3. Olive oil is heated in a pan over medium heat.
4. Finely chopped onions are sautéed until soft and translucent.
5. Minced garlic is added and cooked until fragrant.
6. Tomato puree or crushed tomatoes are added to the pan.
7. Red chili flakes, black pepper, oregano, and basil are mixed in.
8. A pinch of sugar is added to balance the acidity of the tomatoes.
9. The sauce is simmered slowly until thick and rich.
10. Cooked pasta is added to the sauce and tossed gently.
11. Reserved pasta water is added if needed to adjust consistency.
12. Fresh basil leaves are folded in for aroma.
13. The pasta is cooked briefly to absorb the flavors.
14. Finished with a drizzle of olive oil or grated cheese before serving.
