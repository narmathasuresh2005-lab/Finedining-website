Mojito Classic

Mojito Classic is a timeless cocktail that blends fresh mint with zesty lime and smooth white rum. Light, crisp, and incredibly refreshing, it delivers a cooling burst of flavor in every sip. Finished with sparkling soda, it’s the perfect drink for relaxed evenings. A true classic that never loses its charm.

Key Ingredients:

White rum, Fresh mint leaves, Fresh lime juice, Soda water, Simple syrup, Ice cubes, Lime wedge, Mint sprig.

Quantity: 150ml

No. of Orders

Description

1. A tall glass is chilled in advance.
2. Fresh mint leaves are added to the glass.
3. Sugar or simple syrup is added over the mint.
4. Lime juice is freshly squeezed and poured in.
5. Mint is gently muddled to release aroma.
6. Ice cubes are added to fill the glass.
7. White rum is poured over the ice.
8. Soda water is added to top up the drink.
9. Ingredients are stirred gently to combine.
10. Extra ice is added if needed.
11. Lime wedge is placed in the glass.
12. Mint sprig is added for aroma and garnish.
13. Drink is checked for balance and freshness.
14. Glass is wiped clean for presentation.
15. Served immediately, chilled and refreshing.
