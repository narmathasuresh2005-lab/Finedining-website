Chicken Shawarma with Garlic Yogurt

Tender Chicken Shawarma is made with juicy, spice-marinated chicken slow-roasted to perfection. Each bite is soft, aromatic, and deeply satisfying. A classic street-style favorite with a gourmet touch.

Key Ingredients:

Boneless chicken, Yogurt, Garlic, Lemon juice, Olive oil, Cumin powder, Paprika, Coriander powder, Black pepper, Chili powder, Salt, Thick yogurt, Lemon juice, Lettuce.

Quantity: 3 wraps

No. of Orders

Description

1. Chicken is cleaned and cut into thin strips.
2. Yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and spices are mixed to form a marinade.
3. Chicken is coated thoroughly and marinated for several hours.
4. Marinated chicken is roasted or grilled until tender and juicy.
5. Chicken is slightly charred for smoky flavor.
6. Garlic yogurt sauce is prepared by mixing yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, and salt.
7. Flatbread is warmed until soft.
8. Cooked chicken is sliced into thin shawarma strips.
9. Garlic yogurt sauce is spread over the flatbread.
10. Chicken slices are layered generously.
11. Fresh vegetables are added for crunch.
12. Bread is wrapped tightly into a roll.
13. Shawarma is lightly toasted if needed.
14. Extra garlic yogurt is served on the side.
15. Shawarma is served hot and fresh.
