French Onion Dip with French Bread is a rich, creamy classic crafted for effortless indulgence. Slowly caramelized onions are blended into a smooth, savory dip full of deep, comforting flavor.

Key Ingredients:

Onions, Butter, Olive oil, Sour cream, Cream cheese, Garlic, Dried herbs, Black pepper powder, French baguette, Parsley, Salt.

Quantity: 1 full French baguette

No. of Orders

Description

1. Onions are thinly sliced evenly for uniform caramelization.
2. Butter and olive oil are heated together in a pan over low heat.
3. Onions are added and cooked slowly until soft and golden brown.
4. The onions are stirred occasionally to prevent burning.
5. Garlic is added and sautéed gently to release aroma.
6. Caramelized onions are allowed to cool slightly.
7. Cream cheese is softened and placed in a mixing bowl.
8. Sour cream is blended in to create a smooth, creamy base.
9. Cooked onions are folded into the cream mixture.
10. Fresh thyme, black pepper, and salt are added for seasoning.
11. The dip is mixed until rich and evenly combined.
12. French baguette is sliced and lightly toasted until crisp on the outside.
13. The dip is rested briefly to allow flavors to deepen.
14. Served chilled or slightly warm with toasted French bread slices.
