Blue Raspberry Lemonade is a bright and refreshing drink that blends tangy lemon with sweet blue raspberry flavor. Light, fizzy, and perfectly chilled, it delivers a burst of citrusy freshness in every sip. Its striking blue color makes it as visually exciting as it is refreshing. A perfect drink for sunny days and relaxed moments.

Key Ingredients:

Blue raspberry syrup, Fresh lemon juice, Soda water, Simple syrup, Ice cubes, Lime slice, Mint leaves.

Quantity: 150ml

6. Soda water or chilled water is added to top up.
