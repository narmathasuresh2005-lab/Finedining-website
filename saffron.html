Saffron Infused Chicken Biryani is a royal blend of fragrant basmati rice, tender chicken, and handpicked spices. Slow-cooked using traditional dum style, each layer absorbs the richness of saffron and aromatic herbs.

Key Ingredients:

Basmati rice, Chicken, Saffron strands, Yogurt, Onion, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Mint leaves, Salt, Cooking Oil, Coriander leaves, Whole spices, Biryani masala, Red chili powder, Turmeric powder, Ghee, Oil, Lemon juice, Salt.

Quantity: 1 bowl

No. of Orders

Description

1. Chicken is cleaned and marinated with yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice.
2. Marinated chicken is rested to absorb flavors deeply.
3. Basmati rice is washed and soaked for enhanced aroma.
4. Rice is parboiled with whole spices until partially cooked.
5. Onions are fried until golden and crisp.
6. Ghee is heated in a heavy-bottomed pot.
7. Marinated chicken is layered at the base of the pot.
8. Parboiled rice is layered gently over the chicken.
9. Fried onions, mint, and coriander leaves are sprinkled generously.
10. Saffron-infused milk is drizzled evenly over the rice.
11. Additional ghee is added for richness.
12. The pot is sealed tightly for dum cooking.
13. Biryani is slow-cooked on low heat until flavors meld perfectly.
14. Rested briefly before opening to release the aroma.
15. Gently mixed and served hot with raita or salad.
