Fish Fry is a coastal classic featuring fresh fish marinated in aromatic spices and shallow-fried to golden perfection.Crisp on the outside and juicy within, every bite bursts with bold flavor and warmth. A timeless favorite crafted for seafood lovers.

Key Ingredients:

Fresh fish slices, Red chili powder, Turmeric powder, Coriander powder, Black pepper powder, Ginger-garlic paste, Lemon juice, Rice flour, Curry leaves, Salt, Oil

Quantity: 10 pieces

No. of Orders

Description

1. Fish is cleaned thoroughly and cut into even slices.
2. Turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and pepper are mixed.
3. Ginger-garlic paste and lemon juice are added to the spice mix.
4. Salt is added and mixed into a smooth marinade.
5. Fish slices are coated evenly with the marinade.
6. The fish is rested to absorb flavors.
7. Rice flour or semolina is lightly dusted for crispiness.
8. Oil is heated in a pan to medium-hot temperature.
9. Curry leaves are added to the oil for aroma.
10. Fish slices are placed carefully into the pan.
11. Fish is shallow-fried until golden and crisp.
12. Slices are flipped gently to cook evenly.
13. Fried fish is drained on absorbent paper.
14. Served hot with onion rings and lemon wedges.
