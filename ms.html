Margarita Strawberry

4.6 (213 Reviews)

Strawberry Margarita is a refreshing twist on the classic, blending ripe strawberries with smooth tequila and zesty lime. Sweet, tangy, and perfectly chilled, it delivers a burst of fruity freshness in every sip. A perfect choice for relaxed evenings and celebratory moments.

Key Ingredients:

Tequila, Fresh strawberries, Fresh lime juice, Triple sec (orange liqueur), Simple syrup, Ice cubes, Salt, Strawberry slice.

Quantity: 150ml

No. of Orders

Description

1. Serving glass is chilled beforehand.
2. Glass rim is rubbed with lime and dipped in salt.
3. Fresh strawberries are washed and hulled.
4. Strawberries are blended into a smooth purée.
5. Ice cubes are added to a cocktail shaker or blender.
6. Tequila is poured in.
7. Fresh lime juice is added for balance.
8. Triple sec is added for citrus depth.
9. Strawberry purée is added generously.
10. Simple syrup is added if extra sweetness is needed.
11. Mixture is shaken or blended until well chilled.
12. Ice is added to the prepared glass.
13. Cocktail is strained or poured smoothly.
14. Garnished with a strawberry or lime slice.
15. Served immediately, fresh and vibrant.
